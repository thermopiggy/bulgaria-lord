About Us

The “Lord I&G” brand took its first steps on the market in 2000, launched by a small family-owned company producing high-alcohol beverages. The owner and manager of this company is Liliya Gyurova. In the beginning, the company started production with several popular drinks such as rakia, mastika and vodka.

Today the portfolio covers almost all types of high-alcohol beverages. Over the years the company has invested in equipment and production capacity to improve technology in line with EU standards, to create new products and to further develop the “Lord I&G” brand.

For more than 15 years, we have worked consistently to improve the taste and quality of our products and their modern design. “Lord I&G Bulgaria” Ltd. is growing dynamically and successfully, and for more than 10 years has been expanding into the international market with partners from France, Lithuania, Latvia, Belarus and the USA. Interest in our products continues to grow from other markets and international clients.

Grape Rakia 0.7L

Following ancient Bulgarian traditions, “Lord I&G” offers in its portfolio the traditional Bulgarian grape rakia. Produced using a classic Bulgarian method, our rakia is distinguished by its golden colour, smooth texture and refined taste.

Brandy 0.7L

The heart of the “Lord I&G” family is our brandy. The elegant and soft taste of this amber drink will excite even your finest senses. “Lord I&G” brandy offers an exceptional flavour experience with notes of vanilla, cherry pit and almond.

Mastika 0.7L

“Lord I&G” Mastika is a high-alcohol anise spirit with 47% vol., produced by flavouring ethyl alcohol of agricultural origin. It has a fresh and soft taste with rich anise aroma. Best consumed well chilled, forming fine silver crystals.

Vodka 0.7L

The high quality of “Lord I&G” vodka is achieved through the use of premium 100% grain alcohol and pure mountain water from a karst spring at over 1200m altitude. The processing technology includes multi-stage distillation and active-carbon filtration, ensuring its clean, soft and fresh taste.

Gin 0.7L

“Lord I&G” Gin is produced from natural juniper distillate and 100% grain alcohol. It stands out with its exceptionally smooth taste and deep aroma, making it an excellent choice for relaxation and enjoyment.

Brandy 12y.o. 0.7L

A brandy aged 12 years in oak barrels – full-bodied, noble and with a long-lasting finish.
